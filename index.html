About Me

I'm a Unity3D and C# developer with over nine years of experience in game development. I spearheaded the creation of Highstreet Calamity, a multiplayer VR experience, designing systems such as a scalable locomotion engine with 10+ movement modes and real-time multiplayer via Netcode for Entities.

My expertise in the Data-Oriented Technology Stack (DOTS) and VR/AR development has driven impactful outcomes across studios in Egypt, the Netherlands, and Sweden. I hold a Master's in Game Design from Uppsala University Campus Gotland.
